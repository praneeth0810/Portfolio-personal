Career Journey

Jan 2024 - Apr 2025

Research Assistant - Data Engineering

University of Utah, Salt Lake City

Here, I engineered research-oriented data pipelines that emphasized metadata lineage, schema drift detection, and reproducibility. I used Airflow, PostgreSQL, and dbt to build ETL systems that logged schema versions and tracked changes for ML fairness experiments. I also refactored Tableau dashboards by redesigning the data model into proper star/snowflake schemas, improving usability for research teams. On top of that, I mentored undergrad students on data modeling and guided capstone teams through building reliable pipelines.

Jan 2021 - Jun 2023

Data Engineer

Cognizant Technology Solutions, Hyderabad

At Cognizant, I started off validating data pipelines and dashboards for telecom billing systems — basically making sure what we promised the client matched the actual data. Over time, I moved into a hybrid analyst-engineer role, working closely with marketing teams to pull campaign insights, optimize tariffs, and model KPIs. Eventually, I fully shifted into data engineering — building scalable ETL pipelines with Kafka, Spark, and Airflow for fraud detection and pricing analysis. We were handling 1.2TB+ monthly and streaming 15K+ events/sec.
